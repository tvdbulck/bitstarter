FAQ

Do you really make unique jewelry or is this just a way to attract customers?

All our jewels are guaranteed unique. Every jewel is separately 3d printed and uniquely tailored to you, either based on your personal details or on your DNA results.

I don't like to be DNA-tested, can I still order a jewel?

Yes. Jewelry can be uniquified in two ways: through a DNA fingerprint or through your personal data. If you don't like a DNA test, just enter your name and some additional information, and we create a unique 'pDNA' (=personal DNA) fingerprint for you.

Who conducts the DNA fingerprinting tests?

DNA fingerprinting tests are conducted by a professional lab whose core business is DNA fingerprinting: dna-fingerprint.com

How is the DNA test done? Will it hurt?

We can immediately reassure you, DNA testing does not hurt! You will receive a kit to take the DNA sample. This is a really simple: just chew a gum for a few minutes and send the gum back to us. No needles, no pain, just chewing gum ;-)

What will you do with my DNA results?

Nothing. We have no interest in DNA results whatsoever, so normally DNA fingerprinting results are not stored anywhere. However, if you would like to order additional DNA-based jewelry in the future, it might be interesting to still have the fingerprinting results. This will save you a (costly) additional DNA test. Bottom line: it's up to you to keep the results or not.
